To print pages that are displayed on your screen, you'll need to connect a compatible USB printer to your Internet & Media Player.

The following printers are recommended:

  • Epson Stylus C44UX, C64, C82, and C84
  • Epson Stylus Photo 825
  • Hewlett-Packard Deskjet 5150, 5550, 5650, and 6122
