To view your subscriptions, follow these steps:
  1. On the My billing account page, choose View my subscriptions.
  2. Choose the subscription you want to view.
  3. Review the information on the screen.
Your subscription status will be one of the following:
  • Enabled: Your subscription is active.
  • Suspended: Your access to subscription services has been removed. You may not have paid the subscription fee. Do one of the following:

    • Pay the subscription fee.
    • Update the payment method, or transfer the subscriptions to a new credit card.
    • Contact Customer Support:

      • Contacting Customer Support

    • Expired: Your access to subscription services has been removed because the subscription is expired. A subscription can expire if you cancel your subscription within the subscription grace period.

    Notes
    • Choose Renew Subscription to renew the subscription you're viewing.
    • Contact Customer Support to learn why your subscription has expired.
